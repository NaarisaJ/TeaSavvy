About TeaSavvy

Welcome to the TeaSavvy Family! We are a niche, vegan tea company who is focused on conserving the environment and farming sustainably. Out teas are sourced from farmers in the beautiful city of Viaggio, located in Tuscany Italy. Our teas are high quality, fresh and grown naturally. This makes us TeaSavvy.

TeaSavvy Mission

Our mission is to supply our customers with amazing, delicious tea that was grown by farmers sustainably.
