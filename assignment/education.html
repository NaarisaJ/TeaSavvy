EDUCATION:

Tea Benefits

Rose Tea:

has anti-inflammatory properties, its vitamin profile reduces menstrual pains. Studies have shown, women who drink rose tea has reduced menstrual pains, mood swings and anxiety.

Mint Tea:

is excellent for relieving headaches and migraines, unclogging sinuses, improving your sleep and fighting bacterial infections.

Lilac Tea:

is an ancient herb used to treat fevers; it was first used widely in the United States to treat Malaria.

INDULGE & ENJOY:

Some Delicious Ways To Enjoy Our Teas...

Rose Tea:

possesses a sweet subtle flavour. It is mainly enjoyed warmed with honey or warmed with milk and sugar.

Mint Tea:

has a dominant bitterish flavour, to reduce the bitterness milk and sugar is usually added to hot mint tea. During hot summers, chilled mint tea is very refreshing with slices of lemon and cucumbers.

Lilac Tea:

has an “oaty”, herbaceous flavour. It is mainly consumed in the spring, warmed with sugar/honey and in the summer chilled with sugar, lemon and mint leaves.
